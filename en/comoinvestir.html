LET'S TALK

We all know that the financial products that are available in the market are the same ones, what makes us different is HOW TO MAKE the investments. This is the true science. It is where we put OUR BRAIN, our INTUITION and our KNOW-HOW. Tracking this way it is what we, from Brainvest, know what to do best.

We have EXPERTISE and STRUCTURE to attend onshore and offshore. And mainly NOT BEING ATTACHED to any market. Throughout your path, we can change the chosen mix. Before making any recommendation about your estate, it is necessary to give the first step: GETTING TO KNOW YOU.

STEP1

KNOWING YOU

KNOWING TO DEVELOP This is the most important part because we are going to fully understand your financial goals and consequently your personal and professional goals as well.

DURING THIS PROCESS WE WILL ANALYZE

Expectation of the portfolio return;

Client's risk propensity;

Main hindrances and particularities such as issues related to liquidity, family succession, taxes, life expectancy, living standards, dreams and aspirations.
